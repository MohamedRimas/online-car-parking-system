What kind of car parks can I find at Ez-Park?



We currently collaborate with more than 100 car parks in Sri lanka.



Each car park has a personalised file with general information about its operation, characteristics, location, photos, maximum permitted height,

access, available products, height, access, available products and booking validation instructions both on arrival and departure


1.City-Centre Car Parks: Car parks that provide services in city centres.


2.Airport Car Parks: Car parks operating at the main airports. We distinguish Airport Car Parks between


3.Valet car parks: Car Parks with a valet service that will take your vehicle to the car park for you. Vehicle pick-ups and drop-offs are set up at the terminals themselves.


4.Low-Cost Car Parks or Shuttle Service: These car parks operate in the same way as the classic city centre car parks, but also provide shuttle transport to the terminal.


5.Station car parks (Train and Bus) and Port Car Parks: Car Parks operating at the main stations and ports whose services, like airport car parks, can be differentiated between official car parks, valet service or low-cost (shuttle).
