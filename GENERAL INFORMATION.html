What is EZ-PARK?



Ez-Park is Sri Lanka leading website for booking parking spaces online.



We offer products over a range of time periods, (from one hour to annual subscriptions) at unbeatable prices, with discounts of up to 70% off the going rate.



what started as something fictitious became a real company. Since then, our business model has been adapted to customer's needs in order to offer the products they require.
Not only do we help our users to obtain great discounts for their parking, but we also help car parks to maximise the profitability of their infrastructures without any risk.
