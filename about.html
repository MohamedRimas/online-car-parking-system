About Monthly Subscription

Monthly Subcription allows users access to reserve locations for monthly time periods at any hour of the day.

Important

1. Your monthly parking begins once you have made the necessary payment and registered for the subscription. The parking pass issued on the day of registration will be continued for 30 days before expiring. If you wish to continue the subscription, please make sure to renew your parking pass monthly.


*Once subscribed for the monthly payment system, the subscription cannot be cancelled until the nearest renewal date.*


2. The monthly fee will be calculated with the selected parking hours of the month and the vehicle type accordingly. Please note that the fee varies considerably according to your vehicle type.


3.A service fee of 5% will be added to your monthly bill for all services done by the website.


4.It is recommended that you purchase the subscription at least 3-4 business days before the starting date, to allow sufficient time for the facility to process your monthly subscription.


5.Please contact our support service center for further inquiries.
